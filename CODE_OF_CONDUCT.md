# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to a respectful, harassment-free, and constructive community for everyone, regardless of background, identity, experience, or level of expertise.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, patient, and specific.
- Critique ideas and code, not people.
- Assume good intent while remaining accountable for impact.
- Welcome questions and help contributors learn the project.
- Keep discussions relevant and avoid publishing private or sensitive information.
- Accept maintainers' decisions about scope, safety, and project direction gracefully.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, threats, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Sexualized language or unwanted attention.
- Trolling, deliberate disruption, or repeated bad-faith argument.
- Publishing another person's private information without permission.
- Sharing secrets, proprietary source, or sensitive repository data in issues or pull requests.
- Conduct that would be inappropriate in a professional collaborative environment.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to the maintainer through the repository's GitHub contact channels. Reports will be reviewed promptly and handled with appropriate confidentiality. Maintainers may edit or remove content, reject contributions, temporarily restrict participation, or permanently ban participants when necessary.

This policy applies in project spaces and when someone is publicly representing the project.
